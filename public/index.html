The future of our communication start now. Please share the site to help bring these lost punctuation marks into our written language. Together we can stop misinterpretations forever!

For centuries, authors and typographers have been developing special punctuation marks to help bridge the gap and increase clarity. Deemed extraneous and unorthodox, these marks haven’t secured a permanent place in our writing. Today, at a time when email, social media, and texting have defined our written communication, there is an undeniable need to revisit these symbols.

A look back at the future of our communication.

A collection of little-known, non-standard punctuation marks, curated with the goal of bringing them to acceptance in today’s written language.
